# MBAL

Project: Reservoir Performance Analysis – Neelam–Heera Offshore Asset
	•	Analyzed oil, gas, and water production data from 9 wells of the Neelam–Heera offshore field to perform Material Balance Analysis (MBAL) using Excel.
	•	Estimated PVT properties (Bo, Rs, Bg, Rp) using Standing’s correlations and calculated Original Oil in Place (OOIP) and water influx.
	•	Determined drive indices and dominant reservoir drive mechanisms to evaluate pressure support and overall field performance under waterflood conditions.
